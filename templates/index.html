PRESIDENTIAL MODEL UNITED NATIONS

About

Entering its exciting first edition, Presidential Model United Nations (Presidential MUN) is a premier national-level simulation hosted by Presidential Graduate School, targeting high school graduates of Nepal. It offers a platform to step into global diplomacy with debates, resolutions, and workshops.

Guidelines

  • Event Type: Individual Participation
  • Event Scope: National (All over Nepal)
  • Eligibility: High School Graduates
